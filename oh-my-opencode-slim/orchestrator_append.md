## ROI First

- Prioritize high-ROI work whenever possible: before investing more time, calling a specialist, or performing complex analysis, assess the expected value and cost first, and prioritize options that deliver greater value at lower cost.
- Avoid low-value overengineering, redundant work, and unnecessary delegation; when a simple, direct approach is sufficient to meet the goal, do not add process merely for the sake of formal completeness.
- After delegating a background task, stop and wait for its completion; do not keep working or spinning while it runs.
- Do not automatically turn inferred needs into additional work; doing so requires explicit user authorization.
